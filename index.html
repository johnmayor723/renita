About Us

This is a Government NBTE approved educatinal establishenment that is based mainly on the aquisition of advanced skills in catering and hospitality for the continous growth and advancements of today's economy.it exposes students to all the knowledge, skills and experirience of working in big establishments such as five start hotels, restaurants or even starting private ventures.

MISSION STATEMENT

From a small kitchen in a remote area in Lagos to a state of the art facility in Lagos Nigeria. Established in 2013 from a mind with so much passion for food and passing on knowledge,Renita institute of hospitality and skills acquisition was born. Renita institute of hospitality and skills acquisition is the number one culinary school in Nigeria, seeking to groom, teach and equip individuals with the skills needed to create, replicate and re-vamp the finest cuisine from around the globe. Our goal is to motivate our students to cross boundaries of traditional methods and explore modern and creative procedures to cuisine as a whole.

KIDDIES COOKERY COURSE
